EMPOWERING CHILDREN THROUGH EDUCATION

Giving HOPE to Children

An education and the prospect of a career provides children with a path to self-reliance and the opportunity to succeed in life. We provide orphans and vulnerable children in Mombasa, Kenya the opportunity to learn, grow and mature in a healthy and safe environment. These children might otherwise be living on the streets where they are vulnerable to exploitation, radicalization, gangs, drugs and disease.

What We Do

Educational Assistance

We assist children in the form of school and exam fees, uniforms, shoes, books, supplies, backpacks, transportation, and school lunches.

Our Mission

Our goal is to do more than just give our children an education. The children we serve are our family. We want them to be safe and happy. We want them to grow and mature into well adjusted, empowered adults with the skills to succeed in a career, but more importantly, to succeed in life.
