Our Programs

Our programs help to develop children holistically. While we focus on edcuation, help our students grow as persons. We also have a number of programs to help improve the conditions in which our students and their families live.

Educational Services
We assist children in the form of school and exam fees, uniforms, shoes, books, supplies, backpacks, transportation, and school lunches.
